What is Flock

The calendar your whole family will actually use.

Most calendar apps are built for one person. Flock is built for a family. Our signature matrix view puts every member's week side by side — so you can see who's doing what, when, and whether it all fits together.

No more five different apps. No more group texts trying to coordinate pickups. No more "wait, you didn't tell me about that." Just one clear view of your family's life.

Features

Built for how families actually live.

Every feature in Flock is designed for the whole family — not just the person managing the calendar.

🗂
The Matrix View

Flock's signature layout shows every family member's week in a single grid. See everyone's schedule side by side — spot conflicts before they happen.

🎨
Color-Coded Members

Each person gets their own color. At a glance you know whose event is whose — no squinting, no confusion. The calendar learns your family.

🔔
Smart Reminders

Flock sends reminders to the right person at the right time. Calm, plain-language notifications — no noise, no exclamation marks.

🏡
Shared Rows

Add shared rows for things the whole family needs to see: dinner plans, who's driving, grocery days, or your vacation rental schedule.

📺
Wall Display Mode

Mount an iPad in your kitchen and Flock becomes a beautiful always-on family command center. Dark mode optimized. No interaction needed.

⚡
Fast Event Adding

Add an event in seconds — just tap a day and start typing. No forms, no friction. Flock gets out of your way so you can get on with your day.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Flock is designed to be simple to set up and even simpler to use every day.

1

Create your flock

Sign up and add your family members. Give each person a name and a color. That's it — your flock is ready.

2

Add your schedule

Start adding events for each person. Import from existing calendars or start fresh. Flock works however your family works.

3

Stay in sync

Everyone sees the same view. Changes update in real time. Your family always knows what's happening — without anyone having to ask.

What We Believe

Built on four principles.

These aren't marketing words. They're decisions that shape every feature, every screen, and every sentence in Flock.

Together

Every feature is designed for the whole family, not just the person managing it. Flock only works when everyone can see it.

Honest

No clutter, no noise. Just what your family needs to see. We remove complexity rather than adding features for the sake of it.

Warm

Technology that feels human. Interfaces that feel like home. Flock should feel like the most organized person in your family.

Calm

Family life has enough urgency. Flock brings it down a notch. Organized without being clinical. Clear without being cold.
