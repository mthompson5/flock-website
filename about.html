Our Mission

Making family life feel less like chaos and more like choreography.

Most calendar apps are built for individuals. They were designed around the assumption that one person manages one life. But families don't work that way. Families are five people, seven overlapping schedules, a dozen competing commitments — and an endless chain of "did you know about this?" conversations that shouldn't need to happen.

Flock was built from the ground up for families. Not as an afterthought or a sharing feature bolted onto a solo calendar. As a product where coordination is the whole point.

Warm as a kitchen table. Sharp as a well-run household.

"The best family tools feel less like software and more like a second brain — one that remembers everything so you don't have to."

What We Believe

Four principles that shape everything we build.

These aren't marketing words. They're decisions that show up in every feature, every screen, and every sentence in Flock.

01
Together

Every feature is designed for the whole family, not just the person managing it. A calendar that only one person uses isn't a family calendar — it's just a calendar. Flock is only useful when everyone can see it, and we design every interaction with that in mind.

02
Honest

No clutter, no noise. Just what your family needs to see. We'd rather show you five things that matter than fifty that don't. Every feature has to earn its place by making your family's week clearer — not busier.

03
Warm

Technology that feels human. Interfaces that feel like home. Flock should feel like the most organized person in your family — someone who knows everyone's schedule, remembers what matters, and delivers information without drama.

04
Calm

Family life has enough urgency. Flock brings it down a notch. We don't use notifications to create anxiety. We don't design for engagement. We design for confidence — the quiet kind that comes from knowing your week is in order.
