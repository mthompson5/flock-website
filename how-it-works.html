📅 Add events

Add your first events. It takes seconds.

Tap or click any cell in the calendar grid — the intersection of a person and a day — and start typing. Give the event a name, optionally a time. That's all you need.

You can add recurring events (soccer every Tuesday and Thursday), all-day events (school holidays, travel days), and shared events that appear across multiple rows simultaneously.

Common Questions

Things people ask before they start.

Do all family members need their own account?

No. One person creates and manages the Flock account. Other family members can be added as editors or viewers without needing their own login. Kids can appear on the calendar without any account at all.

Can I import my existing calendar?

Yes. Flock supports importing from Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and any calendar that exports .ics files. You can import a family member's existing calendar directly into their row, so you're not starting from zero.

Is my family's data private?

Completely. Flock does not use advertising, does not sell data, and does not share your family's schedule with third parties. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're building a tool for your family — not an audience for advertisers.

Does Flock sync with my existing calendar?

Yes. Connect any iCal feed — Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Airbnb, sports league schedules. Flock syncs automatically in the background. Cancelled events disappear, rescheduled events update, and new events appear without you having to do anything.

What does wall display mode require?

An iPad, a power cable, and a way to mount it. Open Flock in Safari, go to wall display mode, and leave it running. It updates automatically whenever anything changes on any device.

What if two people edit the same event at once?

Flock uses real-time conflict resolution to handle simultaneous edits gracefully. The last saved version wins. You'll never end up with corrupted or duplicated events.
