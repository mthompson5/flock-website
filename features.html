📅 Signature Feature

The Matrix View — every person, every day, one screen.

Instead of toggling between each family member's calendar, Flock puts everyone side by side in a single grid. Monday through Sunday, person by person. You can see at a glance whether Saturday is free or completely packed — and spot that two kids have activities at the same time on Thursday before it becomes a problem.

No more "can you check your calendar and tell me if..." The answer is already on the screen, for everyone.

🎨 Personalization

Each person gets their own color. Instantly readable.

When you set up Flock, you assign each family member a color. From that moment on, you can identify whose event is whose without reading a word. Terracotta for Allison. Sage for Mike. Gold for Michael.

The colors are warm, distinct, and chosen to be easy on the eyes at any hour — especially on a wall display at 7am before coffee.

Colors stay consistent across every device — the same palette on your phone, the browser, and the kitchen iPad.

🔄 Recurring Events

Set it once. Flock handles the rest of the year.

Soccer every Tuesday and Thursday. Piano every Wednesday. Date night every other Friday. Add a recurring event once and Flock repeats it daily, weekly, or monthly. Edit a single occurrence without touching the rest of the series — or update all future events at once.

Recurring events stay in sync with iCal feeds too — if your source calendar updates a recurring series, Flock follows automatically.

🏠 Shared Rows

More than schedules. Anything your family tracks together.

Add shared rows for anything the whole family needs to see — dinner plans, who's driving, grocery days, vacation rental bookings. Each row is color-coded, visible to everyone, and editable by anyone with access.

Common uses: dinner plans, school events, property bookings, chore days, or a dedicated row for family road trips.

Shared rows sit below the family member rows in the matrix view, clearly separated but always visible.

🌎 Multi-Flock View

The only family calendar where families can follow families.

Multi-Flock is something no other family calendar offers. Using an invite code, anyone — grandparents, a co-parent, adult siblings across households — can follow your flock and see your schedule alongside theirs in a single unified view. A flock switcher in the header lets you toggle between flocks or see them all at once.

Grandma wants to know when the grandkids have games? She joins with an invite code and sees Michael's soccer and Sawyer's piano right in her own Flock view. No screenshots, no shared spreadsheets, no "can you send me the schedule again?"

Each flock stays fully independent — separate members, separate events, separate settings. Multi-Flock just bridges them when you want to see the bigger picture. You control exactly what others can see.

📺 Wall Display

Mount it in the kitchen. Leave it running. Never miss a thing.

Flock's wall display mode turns an iPad into a beautiful, always-on family command center. Mount it near the door or in the kitchen — wherever your family naturally gathers before heading out.

Dark mode is the default for wall displays — easier on the eyes in low light, and it looks stunning on a modern screen. Large text, high contrast, zero interface chrome.

Updates automatically. When anything changes on any device, the wall display reflects it within seconds. No interaction needed.
